tajine ingredients

  • 1 kg (2.2 lbs) lamb shoulder or veal, cut into pieces
  • 3 onions, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 2 pinches saffron threads (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley and cilantro
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil or olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon smen (fermented butter, optional)
  • 300 g (10.5 oz) pitted prunes
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Cooking juice from the meat
  • Sesame seeds or blanched almonds for garnish (optional)
